Tips for Beginners

  • 🥾 Start with Easy trails to build confidence and fitness
  • 💧 Bring plenty of water (at least 2 liters)
  • 🗺️ Download maps offline or bring a paper map
  • ☀️ Wear sunscreen, even on cloudy days
  • ⏰ Start early to finish before dark
  • 👥 Hike with friends for safety and fun

What do the levels mean?

Easy: Less than 2 hours, flat or gentle hills, good for everyone
Moderate: 2-4 hours, some climbing, need decent fitness
Hard: 4+ hours, steep or long, need good fitness
